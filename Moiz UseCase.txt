1-USER REGISTERATION AND AUTHENTICATION 

Description: This use case involves the process of users registering on the e-commerce website and 
authenticating their identities.
Steps:

->User visits the website and clicks on the "Register" button.
->User provides required information like name, email address, password, city, etc.
->The system validates the provided information and generates a unique user ID.
->User receives a confirmation email or SMS with a verification link or code.
->User clicks on the verification link or enters the code to authenticate their account.
