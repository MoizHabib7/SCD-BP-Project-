2-Product Search and Purchase:

Description: This use case involves the process of users searching for products 
and making purchases on the e-commerce website.
Steps:

->User enters a search query or selects a product category on the website.
->The system retrieves relevant products based on the search query or selected category.
->User browses through the product listings and selects a specific product.
->User adds the product to their shopping cart and proceeds to checkout.
->User provides shipping and billing information.
->The system calculates the total cost, including taxes and shipping charges.
->User selects a payment method and enters the necessary details.
->The system processes the payment and generates an order confirmation.
->User receives an order confirmation email or SMS.
